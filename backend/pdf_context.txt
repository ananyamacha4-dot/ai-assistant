A Luminary of Indian Cinema
Sridevi, born Shree Amma Yanger Ayappan, remains an indelible force in the annals of
Indian cinema. Her illustrious career, spanning over five decades, saw her evolve from
a child artist to one of the most revered and commercially successful actresses in
Hindi and South Indian films.
Her versatility was a hallmark of her performances. Sridevi effortlessly transitioned
between comedic roles, intense dramas, and captivating dance sequences.
